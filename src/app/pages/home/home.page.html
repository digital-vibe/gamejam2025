Welcome to the official homepage of the GameJam 2025!


Organized by Digital Vibe, the GameJam 2025 runs for exactly one week. GameDevs will use this week to create a game from scratch. On Monday they will use the randomizer below to get their game topics and on Sunday they will have to present a playable game! The game will be played, scrutinized & scored by family & friends using the scoresheet below. Exciting!


Will they be up for the challenge? Or will they perish in the flames of uncertainty. Time will tell...
